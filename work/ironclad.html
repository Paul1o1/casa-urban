The Challenge

Ironclad is revolutionizing the way companies and legal departments use contracts. Their existing brand no longer aligned with how they wanted to be perceived. It felt cold, overly techy and was easily lost within the legal-tech space.

The Solution

Century designed an all new brand identity system that feels fresh, spirited and premium. We also redesigned and developed the company website and provided guidance on how to incorporate the new brand into the product experience.

The Outcome

The new identity was a huge boost for the internal team, who was able to run with the system and extend it into a variety of executions. We continue to partner with Ironclad to design branded experiences, most notably their first Ironclad-hosted conference, Apex: The Modern GC Summit.
